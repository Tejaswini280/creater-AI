- **LinkedIn**: 8:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 5:00 PM, 6:00 PM
- **Facebook**: 9:00 AM, 1:00 PM, 3:00 PM, 6:00 PM
- **Your Timezone**: All times adjusted to your selected timezone (e.g., IST)

### 3. **Automatic Calendar Integration**
- Creates scheduled entries in localStorage
- Each entry includes: content, platform, date/time, timezone
- Entries are marked as "auto-generated" for easy identification
- Automatically navigates you to the scheduler to view results
